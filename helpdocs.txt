Matrix Engine
----

1.1 Scenes

New Scenes MUST Inherit the Scene Class. A "Default" scene is already created for you.

By default, the "Default" scene is loaded on start.

Scene Management is handled by the SceneManager class. A new scene can be loaded by calling loadScene()

com.MatrixEngine.Objects and UIX elements will be created in the load() function of your scene.

----
